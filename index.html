Welcome to Agape

We believe God’s love transforms lives.
Our desire is to see people come to know Jesus, grow in His Word, and live out His purpose in their daily lives.
We are a Christ-centered, Spirit-led, and Bible-based church committed to bringing hope and healing to our city and beyond.
